Canadian Blood Services partnered with The Pie Hole to create a campaign to help give back to the wonderful donors that help each day. The Pie Hole created a give back initiative. Donors receive a voucher to get one free slice of pie at any The Pie Hole location.

Client:
Canadian Blood Services
Brief:
Create digital and print assets for the campaign. This includes social media, in donor centre screenscapes, posters, and vouchers.
Role:
During my placement at Canadian Blood Services, I was tasked with creating the assets for The Pie Hole campaign.
Objective:
The objective of the campaign was to get people excited to donate with a give back partnership. This creates a fun and memorable experience, and donors get to feel even more special with a great slice of pie.

Background

On November 26, 1997, justice Horace Krever tabled a report to the federal government. One of Krever’s key recommendations was the creation of a new national blood authority. Canadian Blood Services assumed full responsibility for the operation of the blood supply on September 28th, 1998. Today Canadian Bool Services has imposed stem cell registry, developed the national umbilical cord blood bank, has a new role in organ and tissue donation and transplants. Since taking over operation of the blood system, their journey has gone from tragedy to trust. They now have one of the safest blood systems in the world.
